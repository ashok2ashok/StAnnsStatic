Philosophy

The faculty of St. Ann's School and College of Nursing believe that a thorough understanding of human mind and body is essential to advocate health and that nurses are responsible for the health of people in the community they work with. Therefore B.Sc Nursing course is offered to equip students of nursing sciences with a solid foundation on various aspects of human body, mind and lifestyle.
